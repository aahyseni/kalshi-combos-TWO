MAJOR UPDATE SINCE THE PRIOR AUDIT
----------------------------------

The final risk report''s conclusion that productive quoting remained blocked by
legitimate enforced caps was superseded. The zero-quote cause was a SQL fanout
in Store.held_positions: fills were directly joined to an RFQ tape containing
up to 12,456 repeated rows per combo before SUM(contracts_centi). This inflated
435 real contracts to 552,393 and ARG-advance delta to -259,302.

The fix now on disk pre-aggregates fills, de-duplicates RFQ metadata, joins the
two one-row-per-combo tables, adds idx_rfqs_market_ticker, and includes a fanout
regression test. Reported verification: 435 correct contracts, ARG delta about
47.7, suite 1789/0, and live quoting recovered from zero to 228+ quotes in about
3.5 minutes on both ARG and ENG flow.

Do not tune caps from pre-fix exposure measurements. Do not repeat that the bot
is still at zero quotes.

P0 — CORRECTNESS AND SAFETY
---------------------------

P0-1. Candidate- and reservation-aware portfolio risk

Defect:
- BookRiskSnapshot contains committed positions only.
- Candidate positions affect analytic caps but not stored ES or P(ruin).
- A concentrating candidate can pass using the safer old book; a balancing
  candidate receives no MC credit in its own decision.

Implement:
- Evaluate committed and post-candidate P&L on common sampled states.
- Include outstanding reservations and simultaneously executable accepts.
- Support candidates introducing new games.
- Compute pre/post EV, model ES99, P(ruin), deterministic loss, and gross.
- Confirm only when candidate EV is positive, post-risk budgets pass, and all
  analytic/gross/burst controls pass.
- Do not accept negative-EV hedges without an explicit enabled hedge-cost budget.

P0-2. Book generations and immediate invalidation

Defect:
- A snapshot can remain time-fresh for about 15 seconds after fills change the
  portfolio.

Implement:
- Add ExposureBook generation; increment on position, settlement, rehydration,
  reconciliation, reservation, and relevant quote mutations.
- Store input_generation in BookRiskSnapshot.
- Invalidate on fills and settlements.
- Publish async results only when their generation remains current.
- Time age is a secondary guard, not the consistency proof.

P0-3. Separate ES from deterministic maximum loss

Defect:
- operative_es=max(model ES, challenger ES, deterministic maximum).
- The deterministic maximum normally dominates all sampled ES by construction,
  reducing the CVaR gate to a premium-at-risk cap.

Implement separate fields and gates:
- production_es_99_cc;
- challenger_es_99_cc by scenario;
- governing_model_es_99_cc=max(production, challengers);
- deterministic_max_loss_cc.
- Gate model ES and deterministic maximum independently.
- Remove or rename operative_es_99_cc so maximum loss is not called ES.

P0-4. Usable MC without hiding unmodeled holdings

Defect:
- Portfolio MC fails the whole book UNKNOWN when any held marginal is absent.
- Rehydration skips gated-off positions to avoid poisoning WC quoting, removing
  their real whole-account risk.

Implement:
- Rehydrate every exchange-held position regardless of quote eligibility.
- Distinguish quote_eligible, risk_modeled, and conservatively reserved.
- Always include exact premium loss, gross, and known concentration.
- Preferred: subscribe/fetch held-leg books. Interim: deterministic unmodeled
  reserve outside model ES. Never score missing marginals as ordinary p=0.5.
- Missing held data must not poison unrelated candidate decomposition or vanish
  from global capital accounting.

P0-5. Exact exchange-quantity reconciliation

What remains after fanout fix:
- Exchange positions identify open tickers, while local fills still determine
  reconstructed quantity.

Implement:
- Exchange ticker, side, position_fp, and subaccount are authoritative.
- Local fills supply cost basis, legs, fees, and provenance only.
- On local/exchange mismatch, block quoting or reserve the larger exposure and
  emit a dedicated reconciliation halt/no-quote reason.
- Exclude settled/zero positions; pin all account endpoints to one subaccount.
- Reconcile unexpected opposite-side/manual holdings explicitly.

P0-6. Fractional contracts in MC

Defect:
- max(1, centi_contracts // 100) turns 37.27 into 37 and 0.40 into 1.

Implement:
- Preserve integer centi-contracts through ComboPosition or convert exactly by
  dividing by 100 in simulator arithmetic.
- Remove the forced one-contract minimum.
- Prove analytic/simulated max-loss parity.

P0-7. Structural/fallback same-game dependence

Defect:
- Structural scoreline legs and copula-only corners/cards are sampled separately,
  discarding same-game cross-block dependence.

Implement:
- Preferred: condition fallback legs on the structural game state/shared factor.
- Interim: structural-split and full-copula challengers, gate on worse tail.
- Do not claim exact all-leg hedging until the bridge exists.

P0-8. Challenger correlation scope

Defect:
- _inflate_corr changes all off-diagonals, including cross-game 0 -> 0.5.
- Universal positive correlation is not always conservative for hedged books.

Implement:
- Inflate only intended same-game pairs with a game mask.
- Preserve cross-game values.
- Add separate named cross-game regime scenarios if required.

P0-9. Directional-cap hedge semantics

Current state:
- skip_directional_cap is the largest legitimate blocker post-fanout.
- analytic deltas are independence proxies and do not fully recognize opposing
  advance hedges.

Implement carefully:
- Do not simply raise delta limits.
- Define them as monotonic hard directional/model-sensitivity backstops.
- Prototype mutex-aware direction on the actual ENGARG book.
- Prove the all-accepted snapshot dominates every realizable accepted subset.
- If richer credit breaks monotonicity, keep a loose hard delta backstop and
  award richer hedge credit only through candidate-aware MC.

MANDATORY TESTS
---------------

Fanout and reconstruction:
- Repeated RFQ rows never multiply quantity.
- Multiple fills plus repeated RFQs preserve exact weighted cost/quantity.
- Conflicting leg definitions fail reconciliation.
- Production-like fanout remains performant with the new index.

Candidate risk:
- Concentrating candidate crosses ruin/ES budget and declines.
- Balancing candidate lowers post-risk and can pass.
- Negative-EV hedge declines absent explicit authorization.
- New-game candidate and outstanding reservations are included.

Snapshot consistency:
- Fill, settlement, rehydration, and reconciliation increment generation.
- Old-generation results are discarded.
- Multiple fills cannot reuse pre-fill risk.

Unmodeled holdings:
- Gated holdings remain in global deterministic/gross risk.
- Missing held marginal does not poison unrelated candidate decomposition.
- Missing marginal never becomes an ordinary usable p=0.5 MC score.

Fractional quantities:
- Exact P&L at 0.01, 0.40, 1.25, 37.27, and 100.99 contracts.
- Analytic and simulated maximum losses match.

Mixed dependence and challengers:
- Corner covariance with ML/total/spread/advance follows calibration.
- Unsupported coupling invokes conservative challenger.
- Same-game inflation leaves cross-game rho unchanged.
- Include a book where indiscriminate positive correlation reduces tail risk.

ES/stress:
- Model ES and deterministic maximum remain distinct.
- Hedge lowers model ES without lowering the deterministic backstop.
- Each gate fires independently.

Directional/mutex:
- ARG concentration increases direction; ENG balance gets justified credit.
- All-accepted bound dominates every accepted subset.
- Unproven multiple-ME structure fails closed.

Reconciliation:
- Exact match, exchange smaller/larger, missing local fill, manual trade,
  opposite side, settled ticker, wrong subaccount, and conflicting legs.

Capital accounting:
- Current equity plus incremental terminal P&L reconciles without double count.
- Deposit/withdrawal and stale marks behave fail-closed.
