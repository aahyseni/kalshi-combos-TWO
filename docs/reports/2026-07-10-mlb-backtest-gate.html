Honest findings (survivors of the correction)

RETRACTED: the v1 "ML×2 18.3¢ gap" was the look-ahead artifact. Honest ML×2: med |err| 0.46¢, bias −0.49¢, 96% within-2¢ (n=548). What survives: the lookahead A/B on fresh data reproduces the phantom (+5.93¢ ML×2 bias under the old policy) — the artifact, not the market, was the story.
Surviving real findings: (1) sub-2¢-fair longshot parlays settle ~0.3% but clear ~8¢ — takers overpay for lottery tickets, the profitable sell-side flow; (2) the mlb_runs structural grid carries its own error (fixed-variant cell 1.20¢ med, but lookahead-inflated cells flagged a calibration follow-up); (3) fixed-run prop cells sit at −0.5 to −2.5¢ bias = markup-shaped, no pickoff signal (HR+ML n=42: −0.59¢; HIT+ML n=89: −1.07¢ — the v1 scare numbers were contaminated).
In-play-only combos excluded: 5,123. The recorder's combo-trades poller sees ~3% of traded MLB combos — clearings are tape-sourced (complete) for exactly this reason. Snapshot policy: last PRE-cutoff snapshot (the fix); 824 printed combos have no pregame snapshot at all and are correctly absent.

Method

gather: MLB-strict combos + marginal snapshots from the recorder DB (read-only, bounded chunks) · clearings from Kalshi's authed trade tape (poller-gap-immune) · per-leg settlement/expiry from the public API · strictly-pregame filter keeps prints before min-over-legs(expected_expiration − 4h). price: blind driver over the LIVE pricing modules + shipped config; the legacy config is a runtime deep-copy override (engine untouched, CLAUDE.md rule 8); fairs computed only for tape-printed combos via a prices-free ticker list (selection cannot bias a fair). analyze: this page.
